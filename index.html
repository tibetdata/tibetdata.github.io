Motivation

For any data science projects, around 80% of the time is spent on data munging or wrangling (i.e. acquiring and cleaning data sets and changing its format). Based on personal experience, it can be especially challenging to get Tibet related datasets.

This site will be a collaborative effort to collect Tibet-related datasets - for both political advocacy and community building. I hope we can help each other.
